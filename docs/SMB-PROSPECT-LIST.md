## üîç HOW TO FIND MORE SMBs

### LinkedIn Sales Navigator Search (if you have it)
```
Company headcount: 51-200 OR 201-500
Headquarters: Colorado OR New York
Industry: Technology, Software, Financial Services
Recent growth: Yes
Currently hiring: Yes
```

### Job Board Mining
1. Go to greenhouse.io/job-board or lever.co jobs
2. Search "Colorado" or "Denver" 
3. Look for companies with 5-20 open roles (indicates scaling)
4. Check company size on LinkedIn

### Tech Lists to Mine
- **Colorado:** Denver Business Journal "Fastest Growing Companies"
- **NYC:** Crain's "Fast 50" or "Best Places to Work"
- **Both:** Inc 5000 filtered by state
- **Both:** BuiltIn "Best Places to Work" (smaller companies)

### Greenhouse/Lever Customer Signals
Companies using Greenhouse or Lever are more likely to use AI tools:
- Check their careers page URL (jobs.greenhouse.io/[company])
- These companies care about hiring tech

## üéØ IDEAL SMB PROFILE

**Best targets:**
- 75-300 employees (big enough to use tools, small enough to lack compliance team)
- Series A-C funded (has budget, is scaling)
- Hiring 5+ roles currently (active pain point)
- Tech or healthcare industry (more likely to use AI tools)
- Colorado or NYC HQ/hiring (regulatory pressure)

**Signals they use AI hiring tools:**
- Greenhouse or Lever careers page
- Job posts mention "assessment" or "video interview"
- Using HireVue, Pymetrics, Paradox (check job descriptions)
- Fast-growing with high-volume roles

**Red flags (skip these):**
- Under 30 employees (probably not using AI tools yet)
- Over 500 employees (has internal compliance)
- No current job openings (not actively hiring)
- Founder still doing all hiring (too small)
