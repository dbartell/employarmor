**Why small companies for pitch practice:**
- Talk directly to owner/founder
- Faster yes/no (no committees)
- Real feedback on messaging
- Lower stakes to iterate
- Can close in 1 call

## üéØ WHY SMALL COMPANIES ARE PERFECT FOR PITCH PRACTICE

| Factor | Small (20-100) | Mid (100-500) | Large (500+) |
|--------|----------------|---------------|--------------|
| Decision maker | Owner/founder | HR Director | Committee |
| Time to decision | 1-2 calls | 2-4 weeks | 2-6 months |
| Feedback quality | Direct, honest | Filtered | Corporate speak |
| Price sensitivity | Higher | Medium | Lower |
| Urgency | Can feel pain | Distributed | Delegated |

**The play:**
1. Practice pitch on 20 small companies
2. Learn what resonates
3. Refine messaging
4. Scale to mid-market with proven pitch

## üîç HOW TO FIND SMALL COMPANY OWNERS

**LinkedIn:**
- Search "[Company name]" + "Owner" OR "Founder" OR "President"
- For staffing: often the owner is the main face
- For healthcare: look for "Practice Manager" or "Administrator"

**Google:**
- "[Company name] owner Denver"
- Often find local news mentions, chamber of commerce profiles

**Direct approach:**
- Small companies = call the main number
- "Hi, who handles HR/hiring? Can I speak with them?"
- Often get the owner directly
